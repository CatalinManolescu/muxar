seevl.fm

seevl is a graph of music entities. Entities are identified by a unique URI (together with a short version, recorded as an ID), and can have different types, such as artist, record label, music genre, etc. They have different properties (influences, genres played, band members,etc.) and are inter-connected through relationships.

Documentation
API

Examples
100 entities playing punk-rock: http://data.seevl.fm/entities/?genre=BntvuZAy&_maxres=100
All entities playing punk-rock and signed on Epitaph Records: http://data.seevl.fm/entities/?genre=BntvuZAy&label=B3ZFtGrJ
Entities (artists) related to Social Distortion: http://data.seevl.fm/entities/RkMQZrfG/related
Entities (groups) related to Glam-rock: http://data.seevl.fm/entities/JhMrUMw9/related
